## **The <span style="background: linear-gradient(to right, #FF6A1A, #FF8A4A); -webkit-background-clip: text; -webkit-text-fill-color: transparent; background-clip: text;">Core Challenge</span>**

<br />

Here's the hardest problem we've solved so far:

<br />

> **How do you maintain brand context across unlimited AI conversations?**

<br />

Every time you chat with ChatGPT, you're limited by how much information can fit in the context window. 

<br />

For quick questions, fine. But for deep marketing strategy across multiple campaigns? You hit the wall fast.

<br />

**Our Solution:**

<br />

We built a decision memory system that intelligently ranks and retrieves the most relevant context for each conversation. 

<br />

Your brand profile, past decisions, project briefsâ€”Mark knows what matters for THIS conversation and brings it in automatically.

<br />

**Result:** Conversations that actually remember your brand without performance issues.
